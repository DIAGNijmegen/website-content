## Visiting researchers
In April, Tanja Lossau visited DIAG, strengthening the already existing collaboration between Fraunhofer MEVIS and Radboudumc. On the 23th of April, she presented about her work at Fraunhofer MEVIS, giving us a clear insight in their organization and research work. We thank Tanja for coming to DIAG!
