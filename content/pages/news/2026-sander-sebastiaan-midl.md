Sander Moonemans and Sebastiaan Ram were awarded the Best Spotlight Award at the [Medical Imaging with Deep Learning (MIDL) 2026](https://2026.midl.io/) conference for their spotlight presentation on Democratising Pathology Co-Pilots. The 3-minute video about their research was showcased to the full conference audience in the main hall.

Their research is described in the MIDL 2026 paper `Democratising Pathology Co-Pilots: An Open Pipeline and Dataset for Whole-Slide Vision-Language Modelling', which is available in the conference proceedings [here](https://proceedings.mlr.press/v315/moonemans26a.html). A recording of the award-winning presentation can be viewed on YouTube, starting at 1:32:20, [here](https://www.youtube.com/live/kXXQ5yVJcTI).
