The CARA Lab will be present at the American College of Cardiology (ACC) 75th Annual Scientific Session & Expo, taking place March 28â€“30, 2026 in New Orleans, Louisiana.

During the conference, Ruben van der Waerden and Rick Volleberg will present our latest innovative research on artificial intelligence for intracoronary OCT.

[member/ruben-van-der-waerden] will present his abstract entitled "Artificial Intelligence for Intracoronary Optical Coherence Tomography: Enabling Real-Time Plaque Segmentation". In this work, he addresses the limitations of complex and therefore slow AI models by investigating a lightweight approach capable of real-time, full-vessel segmentation of intracoronary OCT pullbacks. This research represents an important step toward practical AI-assisted interpretation during cath lab procedures. In a poster session, [member/rick-volleberg] will show a comprehensive validation study of the real-world generalizability of our [OCT-AID](https://academic.oup.com/ehjdh/advance-article/doi/10.1093/ehjdh/ztaf021/8078941) model, providing external validation of OCT-AID performance on a large external dataset.
