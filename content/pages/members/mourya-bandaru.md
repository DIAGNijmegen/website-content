Mourya Bandaru is a second-year Master's student in Data Science at Radboud University. He holds a bachelor's degree in computer science and joined the CARA Lab in February 2026 to work on his thesis. His research involves developing a deep learning pipeline for detecting and segmenting cholesterol crystals and macrophages in OCT images, contributing to [OCT-AID](https://www.cara-ai-lab.nl/news/cara_paper_multiclass/), a tool for automated segmentation of coronary OCT imaging. He is supervised by [member/thijs-luttikholt] and [member/jos-thannhauser].
