She obtained her medical degree (MD) from Baylor College of Medicine in the USA as a member of the MD/PhD Medical Scientist Training Program. She completed her PhD thesis on 3D printing of biomaterials for bone tissue engineering with the Department of Bioengineering at Rice University in the USA.
