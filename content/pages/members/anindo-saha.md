Anindo Saha is a Postdoctoral Researcher working on artificial intelligence (AI) systems for oncology and infectious diseases within the [Diagnostic Image Analysis Group (DIAG)], under the supervision of [member/henkjan-huisman]. As of 2026, he serves as a member of the [American College of Radiology: PI-RADS AI Subcommittee](https://www.acr.org/Clinical-Resources/Clinical-Tools-and-Reference/Reporting-and-Data-Systems/PI-RADS) and supervises the Ph.D. of [Joel Jacob Tomson](https://www.diagnijmegen.nl/people/joel-jacob-tomson/) within the [AI4AI](https://www.nwo.nl/en/researchprogrammes/perspectief/perspectief-grants/ai4ai-artificial-intelligence-for-accessible-medical-imaging) consortium. 

In 2020, he obtained his Joint MSc in [Medical Imaging and Applications (MaIA)](http://maiamaster.udg.edu/) from the University of Girona (Spain), UNICAS (Italy) and the University of Bourgogne (France), as a recipient of the [European Union: Erasmus+ EMJMD scholarship](https://ec.europa.eu/programmes/erasmus-plus/opportunities/individuals/students/erasmus-mundus-joint-master-degrees_en). During this period, he completed a research internship at the Carl E. Ravin Advanced Imaging Laboratories at [Duke University Medical Center (USA)](https://www.dukehealth.org/locations/duke-university-medical-center).

Between 2020-2025, Anindo’s Ph.D. was conducted at Radboud University Medical Center (the Netherlands) and embedded within the [European Union Horizon 2020: ProCAncer-I project —a consortium of 20 partners investigating artificial intelligence systems to support the diagnostic pathway of prostate cancer management in clinical practice](https://cordis.europa.eu/project/id/952159). His research was supervised by [member/henkjan-huisman] and [Jurgen Fütterer](http://magic.radboudimaging.nl/index.php/Person?name=Jurgen_F%C3%BCtterer), and conducted in close collaboration with [Maarten de Rooij](https://www.radboudumc.nl/personen/maarten-de-rooij), Jasper J. Twilt and Joeran S. Bosma. Notably, his Ph.D. resulted in the [PI-CAI](https://www.thelancet.com/journals/lanonc/article/PIIS1470-2045(24)00220-1/abstract) and [SCARLET](https://arxiv.org/abs/2508.03762) studies, and directly contributed to the [PANORAMA](https://www.thelancet.com/journals/lanonc/article/PIIS1470-2045(25)00567-4/abstract) study.
