Saikat Roy is a visiting researcher at the [Diagnostic Image Analysis Group (DIAG)](https://www.diagnijmegen.nl/), where he works on advanced deep learning methods for medical image analysis. His research focuses on large-scale supervised representation learning and 3D medical image segmentation, with an emphasis on developing strong, generalizable backbone architectures. He is a PhD Student at the [German Cancer Research Center(DKFZ), Heidelberg, Germany](https://www.dkfz.de/en/) and [Heidelberg University, Germany](https://www.uni-heidelberg.de/en). He has led the development of Transformer and ConvNeXt architectures that have demonstrated strong performance across diverse CT and MR segmentation benchmarks. He was previously a Masters Student at [University of Bonn, Germany](https://www.uni-bonn.de/en/home) and was associated with the [German Center for Neurodegenerative Diseases (DZNE), Germany](https://www.dzne.de/en/).
