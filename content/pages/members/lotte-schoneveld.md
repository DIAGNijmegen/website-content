Lotte is an Artificial Intelligence bachelor's student at Utrecht University, who is writing her bachelor's thesis under the supervision of [member/sebastiaan-ram]. During her 10-week internship, starting 20/04/26, she will focus on investigating and comparing different reasoning segmentation approaches currently applied to digital pathology and general computer vision. The aim is to understand where the performance gains in pathology-specific reasoning segmentation originate, and what the impact of concept-only (agent-based) reasoning segmentation is compared to end-to-end approaches.
