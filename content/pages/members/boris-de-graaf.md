Boris de Graaf is a PhD candidate in the Computational Pathology Group at the Department of Pathology, working under the supervision of [member/jeroen-van-der-laak]. He holds an MD from the University of Amsterdam and brings professional experience as both a medical doctor and advisor. Driven by a strong interest in the intersection of medicine and artificial intelligence, Boris is dedicated to advancing personalised treatment and improving the quality of healthcare. His research is part of the KWF-funded COMMITMENT project and centres on the optimisation, validation and clinical implementation of prognostic and predictive AI models in digital pathology.
