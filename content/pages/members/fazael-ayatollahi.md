Fazael Ayatollahi received his BSc and MSc degrees in Electrical Engineering from [Amirkabir University of Technology](http://www.aut.ac.ir/). He obtained his PhD at Electrical Engineering Department of, [Iran University of Science and Technology](http://www.iust.ac.ir/en). He joined the [Diagnostic Image Analysis Group (DIAG)](http://diagnijmegen.nl) of the [Radboud University Medical Center](https://www.radboudumc.nl/Research/) as a researcher in February 2019 and worked on applying Artificial Intelligence (AI) and deep learning algorithms in breast MRI and digital gastrointestinal pathology. In October 2022, He started working as a researcher on applying AI algorithm in breast photoacoustic images in [Multi-Modality Medical Imaging group (M3I)](https://www.utwente.nl/en/tnw/m3i/) at the [University of Twente](https://www.utwente.nl/).
